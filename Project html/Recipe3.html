Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Paneer 250g
Butter 100g
Yogurt 2 cups
Salt To Taste
Powder To Taste
Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Cooking Instructions

  • 250 grams paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt (curd)
  • 1 tablespoon gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 medium-sized onion, cut into squares (for skewering)
  • 1 medium-sized bell pepper, cut into squares (for skewering)
  • 1 medium-sized tomato, cut into squares (for skewering, optional)
  • Skewers (for grilling or roasting)
